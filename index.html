FSS Student Club Is the Lighthouse of the International Student

FSS Inc. is the first study abroad agency in Vancouver. With nearly 40 years of experience in studying abroad, it has good cooperation with 300 Canadian university colleges, public high schools, and private institutions. It won the approval of the governor of BC, and the China-Canada Cultural Promotion Association. FSS Inc. set up its first China branch in Dalian in 2018 to provide the best study options and the widest selection of institutions for international student. FSS Inc. also has the Canadian Registered Immigration Adviser (ICCRC member) to provide the most professional and authoritative visa services and immigration consultation for students and their parents. Our company also has formed “student club”, which provides exclusive offers, organizes various campus tours, and holds exciting seminars. For decades, FSS Inc. has assisted tens of thousands of international students to apply for their dream school in Canada. FSS Inc. assists international students from all over the world, including mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Macau, Japan, Korea, and India. FSS Inc. advisors can help students in fluent Cantonese, Mandarin, English and Japanese.
